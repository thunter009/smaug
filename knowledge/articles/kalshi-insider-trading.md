# Kalshi CEO Struggles on Insider Trading Question

Regulatory and compliance concerns raised about prediction market operations.

**Source:** Squawk Box (CNBC)
**Date:** February 10, 2026
**Reported by:** Joon Lee

## Overview

Kalshi CEO Tarek Mansour appeared on CNBC's Squawk Box and struggled to explain why someone with insider information (a Bad Bunny dancer who watched rehearsals) betting on Halftime Show prediction markets wouldn't be considered insider trading.

## Key Points

- Bad Bunny dancer watched halftime show rehearsals
- Subsequently bet on Kalshi prediction markets for the show
- CEO unable to clearly distinguish this from insider trading
- Sports betting dominated Kalshi activity (90% during NFL season)

## Implications

Raises questions about:
- Prediction market regulation
- Insider information handling
- Market integrity and fairness
- Compliance frameworks for betting platforms
